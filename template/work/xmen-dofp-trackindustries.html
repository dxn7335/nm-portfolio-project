How do you excite a skeptical fan base, launch the fifth installment to the X-Men franchise and stand out at a convention that’s over-saturated with the year’s top entertainment titles?

...It starts with a giant robot. To get audiences excited about "X-Men: Days of Future Past," we created a comprehensive launch campaign that included a giant Sentinel head at Comic-Con, a cache of online content built around the film's evil megacorporation, and a corporate lifestyle commercial that established the company’s sinister agenda.
